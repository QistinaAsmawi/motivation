MOTIVATION FACTOR TOWARDS ONLINE EDUCATION

My life goal is to: Get a better grade and score in upcoming exam even using online education

Five (5) factors:

1. Empowerment - By empowering, they can engage students further in learning, provide a more democratic learning experience and, find the most powerful resource in classroom online education.

2. Success - Students need consider in a few key areas such as time managment, learning strategies and self care in order to success.

3. Interest - There are two types of interest that contribute to student motivation which are individual interest and situational interest. Individual interest refers to a focus on the needs or desires of one's self while situational interest refers to an aspect of a course that is enjoyable or fun.

4. Usefulness - Course that is useful and relevant to the students will indirectly contribute to their success in a chosen career field.

5. Caring - Caring instructor will be is a big motivator for students and it would play a large role in online education.

Here is a quote from Dr. Marcus Specht, Professor of Advanced Learning Technologies, Open University of Netherlands

The students of the future will demand the learning support that is appropriate for their situation or context. Nothing more, nothing less. And they want it at the moment the need arises. Not sooner, not later. Mobile devices will be a key technology to providing that learning support
